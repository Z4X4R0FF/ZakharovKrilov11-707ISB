<table class="infobox biography vcard" style="width:22em"><tbody><tr><th colspan="2" style="text-align:center;font-size:125%;font-weight:bold"><div class="fn" style="display:inline">Jeremy Ashkenas</div></th></tr><tr><th scope="row">Nationality</th><td class="category"><a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Known&#160;for</th><td><a href="/wiki/Coffeescript" class="mw-redirect" title="Coffeescript">Coffeescript</a>, <a href="/wiki/Backbone.js" title="Backbone.js">backbone.js</a>, <a href="/wiki/Underscore.js" title="Underscore.js">underscore.js</a>, <a href="/wiki/DocumentCloud" title="DocumentCloud">DocumentCloud</a></td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Awards</th><td><a href="/wiki/Gerald_Loeb_Award" title="Gerald Loeb Award">Gerald Loeb Award</a> <br /> 2015                  <br /></td></tr><tr><th scope="row">Website</th><td><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.ashkenas.com/">ashkenas.com</a></td></tr></tbody></table>
<p><b>Jeremy Ashkenas</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Computer_programmer" class="mw-redirect" title="Computer programmer">computer programmer</a> known for the creation and co-creation of the <a href="/wiki/CoffeeScript" title="CoffeeScript">CoffeeScript</a> and <a href="/wiki/LiveScript" title="LiveScript">LiveScript</a> <a href="/wiki/Programming_languages" class="mw-redirect" title="Programming languages">programming languages</a> respectively, the <a href="/wiki/Backbone.js" title="Backbone.js">Backbone.js</a> <a href="/wiki/JavaScript" title="JavaScript">JavaScript</a> <a href="/wiki/Software_framework" title="Software framework">framework</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Underscore.js" title="Underscore.js">Underscore.js</a> JavaScript <a href="/wiki/Library_(computing)" title="Library (computing)">library</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> While working in the graphics department at <a href="/wiki/The_New_York_Times" title="The New York Times">The New York Times</a>, he shared the 2015 <a href="/wiki/Gerald_Loeb_Award" title="Gerald Loeb Award">Gerald Loeb Award</a> for Images/Graphics/Interactives.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> After working at the Times, he was an employee of Observable, Inc. As of 2020, he works at <a href="/wiki/Substack" title="Substack">Substack Inc</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
