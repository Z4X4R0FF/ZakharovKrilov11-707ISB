	<h1 id="firstHeading" class="firstHeading" >Elon Gasper</h1>
	<div id="bodyContent" class="mw-body-content">

		<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><div class="mw-parser-output"><div class="shortdescription nomobile noexcerpt noprint searchaux" style="display:none">American businessman</div>
<table class="infobox biography vcard" style="width:22em"><tbody><tr><th colspan="2" style="text-align:center;font-size:125%;font-weight:bold"><div class="fn" style="display:inline">Elon James Gasper</div></th></tr><tr><th scope="row">Born</th><td>1952</td></tr></tbody></table>
<p><b>Elon James Gasper</b> (born 1952) is the former Senior VP at VizX Labs<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and co-founder of 1980s era software company <a href="/wiki/Bright_Star_Technology" title="Bright Star Technology">Bright Star Technology</a>. Described by <a href="/wiki/Ken_Williams_(game_developer)" title="Ken Williams (game developer)">Ken Williams</a> as "a genius ex-college professor specializing in linguistics," Gasper holds several patents relating to <a href="/wiki/Lip_Sync" class="mw-redirect" title="Lip Sync">Lip Sync</a> and other technology,<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> and has been active in independent inventors' organizations and their public affairs advocacy.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Work">Work</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Elon_Gasper&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Work">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After Bright Star's 1992 acquisition by <a href="/wiki/Sierra_Entertainment" title="Sierra Entertainment">Sierra Entertainment</a>, Gasper was VP of Educational Games and the driving force behind many notable titles, including genealogy product Generations, traditional game <a href="/wiki/Power_Chess" title="Power Chess">Power Chess</a>, and others.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Personal_life">Personal life</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Elon_Gasper&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Personal life">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Gasper is currently Vice President, Director of Research at <a href="/w/index.php?title=Corum_Group&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Corum Group (page does not exist)">Corum Group</a><sup id="cite_ref-corum_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-corum-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and lives in the Seattle area with his wife.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2013)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p><p>Gasper holds an M.S. in Computer Science and a B.S. in Biochemistry from <a href="/wiki/Michigan_State" class="mw-redirect" title="Michigan State">Michigan State</a> University.<sup id="cite_ref-corum_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-corum-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> He is a successful tournament chess player and occasional puzzle composer.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (August 2013)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
