<p><b>Ryan Dahl</b> (born 1981) is an American <a href="/wiki/Software_engineer" title="Software engineer">software engineer</a> and the original developer of the <a href="/wiki/Node.js" title="Node.js">Node.js</a> <a href="/wiki/JavaScript" title="JavaScript">JavaScript</a> runtime and the <a href="/wiki/Deno_(software)" title="Deno (software)">Deno</a> JavaScript and <a href="/wiki/TypeScript" title="TypeScript">TypeScript</a> runtime.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_life_and_education">Early life and education</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryan_Dahl&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Early life and education">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Dahl grew up in <a href="/wiki/San_Diego" title="San Diego">San Diego</a>, <a href="/wiki/California" title="California">California</a>. His mother got him an <a href="/wiki/Apple_IIc" title="Apple IIc">Apple IIc</a> when he was six years old, one of his first experiences with technology. Ryan attended a <a href="/wiki/Community_college" title="Community college">community college</a> in San Diego and later transferred into <a href="/wiki/University_of_California,_San_Diego" title="University of California, San Diego">UC San Diego</a> where he studied <a href="/wiki/Mathematics" title="Mathematics">mathematics</a>. He went on to attend grad school for Mathematics at the <a href="/wiki/University_of_Rochester" title="University of Rochester">University of Rochester</a> where he studied <a href="/wiki/Algebraic_topology" title="Algebraic topology">algebraic topology</a>, which he found "very abstract and beautiful" for a couple of years but later got bored of it because "it was not so applicable to real life."
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Career">Career</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryan_Dahl&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Career">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>After working on the Node project since 2009, Dahl announced in January, 2012 that he would step away from the project and turn over the reins to <a href="/wiki/Npm_(software)" title="Npm (software)">NPM</a> creator and then <a href="/wiki/Joyent" title="Joyent">Joyent</a> employee <a href="/w/index.php?title=Isaac_Z._Schlueter&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Isaac Z. Schlueter (page does not exist)">Isaac Z. Schlueter</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ryan Dahl gave the following reason for moving on from the project:
</p>
<style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r996844942">.mw-parser-output .templatequote{overflow:hidden;margin:1em 0;padding:0 40px}.mw-parser-output .templatequote .templatequotecite{line-height:1.5em;text-align:left;padding-left:1.6em;margin-top:0}</style><blockquote class="templatequote"><p>“After three years of working on Node, this frees me up to work on research projects. I am still an employee at Joyent and will advise from the sidelines but I won’t be involved in the day-to-day bug fixes.”<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup></p></blockquote>
<p>In 2018 he announced <a href="/wiki/Deno_(software)" title="Deno (software)">Deno</a>, a JavaScript/<a href="/wiki/TypeScript" title="TypeScript">TypeScript</a> runtime built with <a href="/wiki/V8_(JavaScript_engine)" title="V8 (JavaScript engine)">V8</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
