<p><b>Nick Gerakines</b> (born Baton Rouge, Louisiana 1983) is an American author, software engineer, and speaker.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> He is a contributor to a number of notable open source <a href="/wiki/Erlang_(programming_language)" title="Erlang (programming language)">Erlang</a> projects on <a href="/wiki/GitHub" title="GitHub">GitHub</a> and is the author of the book <i>Facebook Application Development</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
